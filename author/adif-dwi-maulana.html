About PyCon ID 2020

PyCon Indonesia is a conference where Python users, or people interested in Python, gather to learn from each other and meet other members of the community. This year, we chose "Connect - Collaborate - Contribute" as our theme. The Pycon ID staff is continuing to work hard on making a conference that will allow everyone to have a great time and unlock new possibilities through Python.

Where

Online, All around the world

14-15 November
